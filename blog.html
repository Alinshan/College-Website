About Our Certificate Programs

Government Polytechnic College, Mananthavady, offers a range of certificate programs in various engineering disciplines. These programs are designed to provide students with specialized skills and knowledge in their chosen field, enhancing their career prospects and employability. The certificate programs are offered in areas such as Computer-Aided Design (CAD), Computer Hardware and Networking, and Electrical and Electronics.

The certificate programs at the US Government Polytechnic College, Mananthavady, are developed in consultation with industry experts and are tailored to meet the needs of the job market. The programs are delivered by experienced faculty members who provide hands-on training and mentorship to students. The college also provides placement assistance to students who complete the certificate programs, helping them to secure employment in their chosen field.

Some of the certificate programs offered by the US Government Polytechnic College, Mananthavady, include:
- Certificate in Computer-Aided Design (CAD)
- Certificate in Computer Hardware and Networking
- Certificate in Electrical and Electronics
- Certificate in Plumbing and Sanitation
- Certificate in Refrigeration and Air Conditioning
